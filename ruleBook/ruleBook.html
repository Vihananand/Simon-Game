RuleBook

  1. When you press key for the first time a box will flash and a sound will be played. Remember the flash and sound.
  2. After the flash and sound are over you have to click the box that flashed for the first time.
  3. Then again any of the 4 box will flash and a sound is played. Remenber it.
  4. Now you have to click the boxes in sequence.

    Eg.if red box flashed for first time and yellow second time, then you have to first click red and then yellow.
  5. Then again any of 4 box will flash. Remember it and click the boxes in sequence.
  6. Repeat the process.
